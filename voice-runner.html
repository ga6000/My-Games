🎤 VOICE RUNNER
Choose Your Game Mode

Two unique ways to control your character with sound:

Voice Mode: Speak naturally. Bright/high sounds go right, dark/low sounds go left.
Whistling Mode: Whistle. Higher pitch goes right, lower pitch goes left.
🎵 VOICE MODE

How to Play

🔊 Loud Volume = Move UP
🔇 Quiet Volume = Move DOWN
🎵 Bright/High Sound = Move RIGHT
🎵 Dark/Low Sound = Move LEFT

Speak naturally - your voice's frequency character controls movement!

🎶 WHISTLING MODE

How to Play

🔊 Loud Whistle = Move UP
🔇 Quiet Whistle = Move DOWN
🎵 Higher Pitch = Move RIGHT
🎵 Lower Pitch = Move LEFT

Whistle clearly - your pitch controls horizontal movement!
